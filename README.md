## Enterprise-QSR-Analytics-Operational-Intelligence-Platform

## Project Type

**Enterprise Business Intelligence & Analytics Transformation Program**

**Industry:** Quick Service Restaurants (QSR)

**Domain:** Retail Analytics, Operations Analytics, Customer Experience Analytics, Workforce Analytics & Financial Intelligence

---

## Project Overview

The Enterprise QSR Analytics & Operational Intelligence Platform is a comprehensive business intelligence ecosystem developed to provide end-to-end visibility across restaurant operations, customer experience, workforce performance, sales performance, inventory management, financial performance, and operational efficiency.

The platform consolidates data from multiple operational systems, food aggregators, sales channels, workforce management systems, audit platforms, and financial systems into a centralized analytics framework.

Built using Power BI, SQL, Python, and automated ETL pipelines, the solution enables leadership teams, regional managers, operations teams, finance teams, and store managers to monitor business performance through real-time dashboards and KPI-driven reporting.

The platform supports more than 250+ restaurant locations and provides actionable insights across multiple business functions through an integrated analytics architecture.

---

## Business Problem

Managing large-scale QSR operations requires continuous monitoring of sales performance, food costs, operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, workforce performance, and profitability.

Prior to implementation, business users faced challenges such as:

- Fragmented reporting systems
- Manual Excel-based reporting processes
- Delayed business insights
- Limited visibility into restaurant performance
- Difficulty identifying operational bottlenecks
- Inconsistent KPI monitoring
- Lack of centralized decision-making dashboards

Leadership teams required a scalable analytics platform capable of delivering real-time insights across all business functions.

---

## Solution

Developed an enterprise-wide analytics platform consisting of multiple interconnected Power BI dashboards and automated reporting solutions.

The solution integrated data from:

- POS Systems
- Zomato
- Swiggy
- Inventory Systems
- Training Platforms
- Audit Systems
- HR Systems
- Financial Systems
- Dispatch Operations
- Restaurant Operations Platforms

Implemented automated ETL pipelines, KPI frameworks, business logic layers, and executive dashboards enabling stakeholders to monitor operational performance, customer experience, profitability, workforce efficiency, and inventory consumption in real time.

The platform delivered a single source of truth for business decision-making across the organization.
